hand–stretched deliciousness

We're Barney and Sammy and we would love to introduce you to Farm Slice, our high-quality, hand-stretched, wood-ﬁred pizza company.

We use the best local ingredients with many from our family farm (here in Nash) – that’s what makes us different and our pizzas extra tasty!

the big idea

We were inspired by all the amazing local produce in England and thought we should combine it with something equally delicious so naturally we thought of that delicious doughy treat - PIZZA!

We spent months perfecting our dough for the bases and topping them with the best fresh produce, then lovingly restored our vintage Rice horsebox into a mean green pizza wagon.

We think you’ll be able to taste the love, thought and effort that goes into each one of our pizzas as we spread our ‘carb-y’ joy all over Milton Keynes and beyond.

what goes into all our pizzas

(aside from the obvious: Love, Passion, Enthusiasm, Effort, Dedication & Time)

The dough

We use the amazing Doves Farm organic ﬂour to make all of our pizza bases in house, its soft, beautifully milled and produces awesome bases.

dovesfarm.co.uk

The cheese

We use Laverstoke Park farm’s buffalo mozzarrella for all of our pizzas. It is honestly the most delicious, creamy mozzarrella we’ve ever tried!

laverstokepark.co.uk

The Toppings

If the produce isn’t from our family farm then its from another amazing English producer. It’s not just opening a packet we take great care preparing the ingredients in the kitchen so they’re perfect for pizzas.

The Tomato sauce

We source high quality tomatoes then slow roast them with olive oil and our secret seasoning to give our pizza sauce the best possible ﬂavour. It is delicious and absolutely never out of a tin!
